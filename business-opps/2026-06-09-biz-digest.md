## First-Time Buyer Tips
- **Demand 2–3 years of filed accounts and bank statements** — never trust headline "turnover" or "profit" figures in a listing; verify against HMRC/Companies House records.
- **Ask whether profit includes the owner's own labour** — many small-business "profits" vanish once you pay someone to do the owner's job, which you must if keeping your day job.
- **Clarify exactly what's included** — stock, vans, tools, domain names, marketplace logins, customer lists and contracts — and get it in the sale agreement.
- **Check licences and insurance before paying** — recovery/transport, care, and waste-related work often need operator licences or DBS/CQC registration you may lack.
- **Confirm customer concentration and transferability** — will clients/contracts stay after the owner leaves, and are any tied personally to the seller?
